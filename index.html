15 mins 230 cals 15 ing

    Ingredients Of Vegetable Biryani

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup onions-grated
  • 1 tsp garlic-ginger paste
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables-finely chopped

    How to Make Vegetable Biryani

  1. 1.1. Heat oil and add cumin seeds.
  2. 2.2. Saute and add the onions, garlic-ginger paste. Saute till brown.
  3. 3.3. Add vegetables, stir fry over low heat till half done.
  4. 4.4. Add coriander powder, garam masala, haldi, salt, chilli powder and green chillies.
  5. 5.5. Cook, covered for about 5 minutes and mix in the lemon juice and half the coriander.
  6. 6.6. The water should be absorbed by now. Remove half the vegetables and layer with half the rice.
